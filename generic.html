About Me

Alec Sinek Claremont McKenna College '22

Dual Degree in Economics and Government. Sequence in Leadership Studies.
Four years of college basketball leadership with experience in Wealth Management, Data Analysis, and Political Campaigns.

I am a recent graduate of Claremont McKenna College. I played Division 3 Basketball for my four years, developing strong leadership skills and a talented network of Claremont McKenna and Harvey Mudd graduates.

As a recent graduate of Claremont McKenna College, and a four-year leader on our basketball team, I am a “glue-guy” with experience in strategy implementation, goal setting and servant leadership. As a Senior Staff Intern at the Reagan Foundation, I was trusted to coordinate the Nancy Reagan Centennial, develop the library’s website on Reaganomics, create an in-person Student Leadership Program during COVID, and schedule leading financial executives and heads of U.S Military branches for the Reagan National Defense Forum. At a digital media startup, I implemented Google Analytics to bolster sales presentations with outdoor retailers like Bass Pro Shops. Additionally, I led a comprehensive online strategy for a heavily contested State Senate Campaign in Phoenix, AZ.

I have lived in Argentina and Sweden. I am an avid outdoorsman and often trek up to the Mammoth Lakes to camp and fish. Although I no longer play collegiately, you can find me playing pickup hoops at whatever court is around.

I started my career as a Financial Advisor, but the company and overall industry did not match my desire for growth. While I excel in sales and relationship management, I want my work to push me beyond my skills. This drive is the motivation for this site, but also the center focus of my professional search. I invite you to reach out and learn more about me!
